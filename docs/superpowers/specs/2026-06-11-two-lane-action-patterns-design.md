# Two-Lane Action Patterns

## Goal

Encourage jumping and ducking from the beginning of a run with readable two-lane obstacle rows.

## Design

Add four tier-zero patterns to the existing curated pattern library:

- ground blocks in the left and center lanes
- ground blocks in the center and right lanes
- overhead bars in the left and center lanes
- overhead bars in the center and right lanes

Each row leaves one lane open, so the player can perform the prompted action or dodge into the open lane. Existing random selection, reachability validation, and spacing remain unchanged.
